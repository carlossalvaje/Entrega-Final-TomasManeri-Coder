Size Guide

To quickly determine your shoe size, stand on a piece of paper, trace the outline of your foot with a pencil, and measure the distance from your heel to the tip of your longest toe in centimeters. Refer to the manufacturer's size chart and choose the size that matches the measurement obtained, taking into account any necessary size system conversions.
